What Works When

REAL
TALK

We believe in being honest. Here's exactly what FitFlow needs internet for — and what runs on minimal or no data.

✅
Works on minimal internet — once logged in
Workout content, GPS tracking, local logging — low data
After your first login, workout content is cached on your device. Exercises, sets, reps, rest timers — all viewable without internet. GPS tracking uses your phone's own GPS chip — no mobile data needed for distance and pace. Logs save locally first and sync to your cloud sheet automatically when you reconnect.
📶
Needs internet — just 3 moments
Login · Map tiles · Instructional videos
🔑 Login (one-time per device)
After first login, your session stays active. Reinstall? Login again and all your data syncs back in seconds from Google Sheets.
🗺️ Map tiles during GPS runs
The street map background needs data to load. No internet = GPS still tracks your route perfectly — you just see a blank background instead of streets. Distance, pace, time — all accurate.
🎥 Exercise demo videos (optional)
Instructional videos open in YouTube. Most exercises have clear text descriptions so you're never stuck. Videos are enhancement, not requirement.
☁️
Cloud backup — automatic, silent
Every run, workout, stat — saved to your own Google Sheet
Every activity you log syncs to a Google Sheet that you own. Reinstall the app, clear cache, switch phones — log in and everything restores: runs, workouts, body stats, weight history, gym weights, calisthenics progress, module order. Your data belongs to you, not us.
